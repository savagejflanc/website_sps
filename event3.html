Science Saturday on the MS Gulf Coast

A Celebration of STEAM Education

September 20, 2025
510 Washington Ave, Ocean Springs, MS 39564
FREE ADMISSION ✨

Event Overview

Get ready for a day of hands-on discovery and innovation at Science Saturday, part of the statewide Mississippi Science Festival! Hosted at the Walter Anderson Museum of Art in partnership with the LeFleur Museum District in Jackson, MS, and other museums throughout the state, this interactive event celebrates the exciting world of STEAM—science, technology, engineering, art, and mathematics.

What to Expect

Families, students, educators, and curious minds of all ages are invited to explore the wonders of science through engaging exhibits, live demonstrations, and activities led by experts and organizations from across Mississippi. From robotics to biology to the art of scientific illustration, discover how STEAM is shaping the future of our state.

WAMA is proud to serve as the Gulf Coast satellite location for this vibrant, statewide celebration of science education and workforce development.
