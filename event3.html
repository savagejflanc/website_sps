Program Overview

Our Physics Outreach Program brings exciting demonstrations and hands-on activities to local schools, inspiring young minds to explore the wonders of physics and science.

Activities Include:

  • Interactive physics demonstrations
  • Hands-on experiments for students
  • Q&A sessions with physics students
  • Career path discussions
  • Take-home physics projects

Target Audience:

  • Middle school students
  • High school students
  • Science teachers
  • School administrators

All materials and equipment are provided by SPS USM.

Get Involved

Schools can request a visit, and SPS members can volunteer to participate in outreach events.
