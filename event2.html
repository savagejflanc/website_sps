Conference Details

Join our SPS chapter as we attend the annual American Physical Society Conference. Our students will be presenting their groundbreaking research and representing USM on the national stage.

Conference Highlights:

  • Student research presentations
  • Networking opportunities with leading physicists
  • Latest developments in physics research
  • Career development workshops
  • Social events with other SPS chapters

Who Should Attend:

  • Students presenting research
  • SPS members interested in physics research
  • Students looking to network in the field
  • Faculty mentors and advisors

Travel and accommodation details will be provided to registered participants.

Registration & Funding

Limited funding is available for student presenters. Early registration is recommended.
